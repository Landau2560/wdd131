Basketball Website - Site Plan

1. Site Name

Site Name: HoopVision Basketball

I chose this name because it gives players and fans a clear and exciting views of everything basketball such as practice drills and training programs.

2. Site Purpose

The purpose of HoopVision is to provide fans and athletes with training program information, different practice drills, and stat information. This website will be used as a hub for everthing related to the sport.

3. Scenerios

  • How can i improve my shooting and ball handeling?
  • What is a good training program to help me progress over time?
  • Where can i track my progress?

4. Color Scheme

Color Scheme For The Project:

Primary Color(#e63946)Used for buttons, selection titles, and highlights.

Secondary Color(#1d3557): Header background, navigation, and footer.

Accent Color(#ffb703):Used for icons and borders.

Background Color(#f1faee):Light, clean background for readability.

5. Typography

Font Selected: Montserrat

  • Headings:Montserrat Bold
  • Body Text:Montserrat
  • Navigation:Montserrat semi-bold
